Privacy Policy

Pre-Launch Version — Waitlist & Early Access | Effective March 2026

1. Overview

EzoraAI LLC ("EzoraAI," "we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit ezora.ai and join our waitlist. We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email address (required to join the waitlist)
  • Name, role, or other information you voluntarily provide in any waitlist form

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring URL (how you found our site)
  • Device type and operating system

This automatic collection occurs through standard web analytics tools including cookies and similar tracking technologies. See Section 5 for more on cookies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you waitlist confirmation and updates about the EzoraAI platform
  • Notify you when early access becomes available
  • Understand how people find and use our website so we can improve it
  • Communicate with you about the platform, including features, pricing, and launch timing
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will only contact you for purposes related to EzoraAI. We will not send unrelated marketing or share your email with third-party marketers.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our website and communications (e.g., email platforms, analytics providers). These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.
  • Law enforcement or government authorities if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of EzoraAI or others.

We will not share your information with any third party for their own marketing purposes.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors use our site. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., form submissions)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand traffic and usage patterns (e.g., Google Analytics)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website functionality. By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described here.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and waitlist information until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until we determine the information is no longer needed. Analytics data is retained in accordance with our analytics providers' standard policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from our emails at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@ezora.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

8. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your information, and the right to opt out of the sale of your information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at privacy@ezora.ai.

9. Users Outside the United States

EzoraAI is based in the United States. If you access our site from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our site, you consent to this transfer.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us at privacy@ezora.ai and we will delete it promptly.

11. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy at ezora.ai with a new effective date. If changes are material, we will notify waitlist members by email.

13. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data: privacy@ezora.ai
