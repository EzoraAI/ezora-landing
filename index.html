Why we’re building this.

Founded by two operators

Backgrounds in Private Equity, Venture Capital fundraising, IT Infrastructure Optimization, and Cloud FinOps. We’ve spent years at the intersection of capital, technology, and business operations.

The question that started everything

We’ve been studying the rapid acceleration of AI — not as spectators, but as operators who see the numbers. And one question kept us up at night:

What happens to the majority of the white-collar workforce when AI can do their job faster, cheaper, and around the clock?

And a second question, just as urgent:

What happens to the millions of small and mid-sized businesses that don’t have the infrastructure, the talent, or the knowledge to stay relevant during a technological revolution?

The honest answer

Neither party — the individual professional nor the average business — truly has the education or the tools to survive this transformation. And it’s not their fault. The AI landscape today is fragmented: knowledge is scattered across social media, tools are siloed, and businesses struggle to identify who to trust. There is no central, structured, reliable place to go.

That’s what we’re building.

EzoraAI is a unified platform where individuals and businesses can discover, access, and leverage trusted AI expertise, tools, and services in one place. We combine the best elements of a professional network, a services marketplace, and a knowledge hub — designed specifically for AI.
