The difference

Built on radical transparency.

The AI talent industry is broken by hidden fees, opaque pricing, and unverifiable credentials. We're building the opposite — a platform where every price, every credential, and every track record is visible, verified, and permanent.

Why transparency matters now

The AI economy is growing faster than trust can keep up. Businesses are spending thousands on experts they can't verify. Individuals are paying for coaching from people with inflated resumes. And platforms are taking 40–60% of every transaction without telling either side.

We believe the AI economy deserves better infrastructure. Infrastructure where you know exactly what you're paying, you can verify exactly who you're hiring, and every expert's track record is immutable and on-chain.

That's not a feature. It's our foundation.

What the industry doesn't want you to see

The current talent marketplace model has structural problems that hurt both experts and the people who hire them.

40–60%

Hidden platform markups

Major platforms fold their margin into the hourly rate without disclosing it. You pay $150/hr — the expert gets $80. Neither side knows the real split. Some platforms even require experts to sign NDAs about their actual pay.

1x

One-time vetting, lifetime badge

Pass a screening test once and you're "top 3%" forever — regardless of what you do after. There's no continuous verification, no proof of recent work, and no way for clients to see real outcomes.

$500

Pay before you even start

Some platforms require a $500 refundable deposit plus a monthly subscription before you can even see a single expert profile. The barrier to entry locks out individuals and small businesses.

0

Zero access for everyday people

Premium talent platforms serve enterprises only. If you're an individual who needs to learn AI to stay relevant in your career, these platforms weren't built for you. You don't exist to them.

How EzoraAI is different

We didn't just build a better marketplace. We built a fundamentally different model — one where transparency isn't a feature, it's the architecture.

01

Published pricing. No hidden fees.

Our take rate is 15–25% and it's published. Experts set their own prices. Users see exactly what they're paying. Experts know exactly what they're earning. No opacity. No NDAs about compensation. No surprises.

02

$8.99 to start. No deposit.

Access the entire expert network for less than a Netflix subscription. No $500 deposits. No enterprise-only gatekeeping. Whether you're a Fortune 500 company or a freelancer learning AI — you're in.

03

Blockchain-verified. Always.

Every project completed, every coaching session delivered, every client rating — written to an immutable blockchain ledger. An expert's reputation isn't a one-time badge. It's a living, verifiable, permanent record that no one can fake.

For people hiring experts

You see verified proof of real work before you spend a dollar. Not self-reported skills. Not a badge from a test they took three years ago. Actual, timestamped, client-verified deliverables. You know what you're getting because the blockchain doesn't lie.

For experts building careers

Your track record is permanent, portable, and impossible to fake. Every great session, every successful project, every five-star review compounds into an on-chain reputation that follows you forever. No platform can take it away. No competitor can copy it.

For the AI economy

Blockchain verification creates a trust layer that the entire industry lacks. It eliminates credential fraud, incentivizes quality work, and makes the marketplace self-policing. Bad actors can't hide. Great experts rise to the top — provably.

Why not just reviews?

Reviews can be faked, deleted, or manipulated by platforms. Blockchain records can't be edited, removed, or altered by anyone — not even us. That's the difference between "trust us" and "verify it yourself."
