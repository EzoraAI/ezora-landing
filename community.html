Why a private forum beats Discord

  • Moderation at scale: Real humans curate conversations, not algorithms. Spam-free, signal-rich.
  • Expert endorsement: Verified AI professionals, not anonymous avatars. You know who you're learning from.
  • Searchable knowledge base: Questions and answers persist. What you ask today helps someone tomorrow.
  • No notifications spam: Quiet by default. You choose what matters to you. No FOMO, no chaos.
  • Structured for AI work: Forums by topic, workflows searchable, projects trackable. Made for builders.
  • Part of your membership: Not another app tab. One place, one community, all included.
